# Capstone 2: EmporiUm Sales Analysis
**Analyst:** Cagla Celik  
**Date:** May 2026

## Project Overview

This project analyzes in-store sales performance across two Northeast territories at EmporiUm, a retail chain specializing in books, technology products, art supplies, and other retail items.

The analysis compares the territories managed by **Miami Vue** and **Bo Heap** to evaluate revenue trends, store performance, customer activity, and product category sales in support of Next Quarter 2026 marketing strategy planning.

**Territories Analyzed**
- Miami Vue — New Jersey (16 stores)
- Bo Heap — Massachusetts (18 stores)

**Data Period:** January 2022 – September 2025

**Tools Used**
- Python 3
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

## Key Findings

- Miami Vue’s territory generated over **$5.1M** in total revenue and showed strong year-over-year growth through 2025.
- **Technology & Accessories** was the highest-performing category and the primary driver of revenue growth.
- **Newark** and **Trenton** were the top-performing stores across the territory.
- The **Paterson** store stopped recording transactions after November 2024 and may require operational review.
- Rewards members spent more per transaction on average than non-members, highlighting the value of loyalty marketing.

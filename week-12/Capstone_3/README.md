# Capstone 3: EmporiUm Northeast Region Sales Analysis
 
**Analyst:** Cagla Celik   
**Date:** June 2026
 
---
 
## Project Overview
 
This Power BI report analyzes EmporiUm sales performance in the Northeast region from 2022–2025. The analysis focuses on sales trends, category performance, state-level revenue distribution, and top-selling books to support data-driven business decisions.
 
---
 
## Data Preparation
 
Data was cleaned and transformed using Power Query by:
 
- Resolving error values
- Trimming inconsistent category names
- Standardizing data types
- Creating relationships between sales, product, and location tables
 
*Note: The Management Team table required additional cleanup, but manager-level analysis was not required for this project.*
 
---
 
## Report Pages
 
### Executive Overview
- Total Sales, Transactions, and Average Sale KPIs
- Monthly sales trend analysis
- Sales distribution by state
 
### Category & Book Analysis
- Sales by product category
- Top-selling books and authors
- Interactive region and year filtering
 
### Insights & Recommendations
- State-level sales concentration
- Category performance analysis
- Reader preference trends
- Strategic business recommendations
 
---
 
## Key Findings
 
- Generated over **$100M** in sales during the reporting period
- Technology & Accessories was the top-performing category
- New York and Massachusetts accounted for a significant share of regional sales
- Science, philosophy, and fiction titles consistently ranked among the best-selling books
